Soit $x_1-x_0 = x_2-x_1 = h$ et le changement de variable $u=x-x_1$:
\begin{align*}
    &\int_{x_0}^{x_2} (x-x_0)(x-x_1)(x-x_2)\,dx\\
    &\qquad = \int_{-h}^h (u+h)u(u-h)\,du = 0
\end{align*}
